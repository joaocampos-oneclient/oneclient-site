## Horizontal AI Is a Commodity

The AI landscape in 2026 looks very different from 2023. Back then, having access to a large language model was a competitive advantage. Today, every developer can call GPT, Gemini, or Claude with a few lines of code.

When everyone has the same tools, the tools stop being the differentiator. What differentiates is how you apply them.

## The Vertical Opportunity

The biggest opportunities in AI are not in building better models — they're in building better systems for specific industries. A healthcare AI that understands medical coding, insurance workflows, and patient communication patterns. A real estate AI that knows market dynamics, lead qualification signals, and agent workflows.

These are not features you can add to a horizontal platform. They're entirely different products that happen to use AI as their foundation.

## Our Approach

We partner with operators who have spent years — sometimes decades — understanding their markets. They know the workflows, the pain points, the regulations, and the opportunities. We bring the engineering capability to turn that knowledge into AI-powered infrastructure.

The result is something neither party could build alone: vertical AI systems that actually work in production, not just in demos.

## What's Next

We're actively looking for the next vertical where this model can work. If you operate in a market with complex workflows, high-value transactions, and data-rich interactions, we should talk.
